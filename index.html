ADB Debugging Instructions

To enable ADB debugging on your Oculus Quest 2:

  1. First, enable Developer Mode on your Quest 2:
    1. Open the Oculus mobile app.
    2. Go to Settings.
    3. Select Devices and choose your Quest 2 headset.
    4. Find Developer Mode and toggle it on.
  2. Next, enable USB Debugging on your headset:
    1. Put on your Quest 2 headset and navigate to Settings.
    2. Select System > Developer.
    3. Toggle the USB Debugging option to on.

Once enabled, you can connect your headset to your computer and use ADB commands.

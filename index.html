Welcome to VBN GTag Modder

Use this tool to mod Gorilla Tag on your Quest 2.

How to Upload Mods

After uploading your mod file, follow these instructions to install it:

  1. Connect your Oculus Quest 2 to your computer or Chromebook.
  2. Click on the Android folder in the connected device.
  3. Navigate to the data/data/com.axos.gorillatag/files/ directory.
  4. Upload your mod file here.

For your convenience, the mod will be prepared for easy copying!

ADB Debugging Instructions

To enable ADB debugging on your Oculus Quest 2:

  1. Plug in your Quest 2 headset to your computer.
  2. Open the Oculus app on your smartphone.
  3. Go to "Settings" in the app.
  4. Select your Quest 2 headset from the list.
  5. Scroll down and toggle "Developer Mode" on.
  6. Once Developer Mode is enabled, connect your Quest 2 to your computer via USB.
  7. Open your computerâ€™s terminal (Command Prompt on Windows or Terminal on macOS).
  8. Type adb devices to check if your headset is recognized. If not, ensure that ADB drivers are installed for your device.

Note: If you encounter issues with recognition, you may need to install ADB drivers specific to your operating system.
